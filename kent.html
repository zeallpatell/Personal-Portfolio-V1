25 Kent is a commercial tenant building owned by Heritage Equity Partners and designed by Gensler. My school partnered with them for a design competition to design a marketing room. The room was required to have a reception, a pantry area and a lounge area.

I wanted to have an open floor plan design. In order to achieve that I designed a two-step high platform which will be the lounge area. The room has two entrances, the east entrace will lead to the reception and the pantry area. The reception area is kept clean with only the desk, some storage space and the pantry cabinates. The reception also has a model to the building to attract potential clients. As you climb up the two steps, we get into the lounge area. The couch sitting provides a nice area to have conversations and the interactive table top in the middle can be used to show floor plans. There is also a T.V which can be used for presentations. All in all, I wanted this space to be modern in feel and promote openess.
