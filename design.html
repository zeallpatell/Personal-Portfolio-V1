Every architect's dream work space. This space has all the tool an architect may require to design the workstation. Health is also a major concern when it comes to sitting in front a screen for long hours. For that the desk is a motorized sit/stand desk. So when ever you feel stiff just press a button and you can continue doing your work. And, for long sitting hours the ergonomic chair helps your back.

On the other side of the room, there is a drafting table which also has a computer just incase you need it for referencing something. The table is made from high quality wood and steel. What happens when you sketch a model on paper but want a physical copy of it? This room also has a 3D printer so you can print it straight from your workstation or your computer at the drafting table. No need to find a maker space or anything.
