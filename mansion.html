Luxury. One word kept in mind throughout the design process of this mansion. It is a three story modern styled mansion which is situated on a beautiful lot. The exterior of the mansion boasts a simplistic smooth white stuco with a red brick facade to give a modern feel. The design style also features many curves which smoothly flows from one part of the house to other. For instance, the ceiling of the master bed curves up and the wall curves out to provide an even more expansive feel.

Let's talk about amenities. On the first floor there is a four car garage and an arcade room. There isn't a better place to have some friends over and have some fun. It house a two lane bowling alley, pool tables, air-hockey, ski-ball, arcade machines and pinball machines. The second floor is the main floor which is accessed by the grand exterior stairs and on the second floor there is a chef's kitchen, formal dining room, living room, study & library and a guest bedroom. As we climb up the stairs to the third floor, the left third of the floor is the master suite. On the other side of the floor the rest of the four bedrooms are located each with an onsuite. It is truely the ultimate living experience money can buy.
