This is a concept living room I deisgned in AutoCAD and Rhino. This space is more centered towards movie nights and entertainment. The star of the show (no pun intended) in the 75" samsung 4k LED T.V. A great video experience needs to be paired with an equally great audio experience and for that the combination of the sound bar with floor standing speakers provide the best movie experience.

An entertainment space is incomplete with a gaming console. Currently the space has both a PlayStation and an Xbox, but the T.V. console has plenty of space to add in more consoles. So, no matter what there is always a choice available to play. One of the other key features of this room is the tree shaped shelf. This uniqe shelf provides plenty of space to store everthing from Blurays, to video game CDs to figurines to any other decorations. In summary, I designed this space to just relax and entertain.
