Who would not love to come home on a friday evening and play video games all weekend? Although with a gaming room like this it might be hard to resist till the weekend. The center feature of this room is the gaming station which has all the lastest devices a gamer could wish for. On one side of the L-shaped desk is the multiple monitor setup which is connected to a water cooled gaming pc. The setup also has the best in class sound system so you can feel every bang of a gun fired or every whroom of drift. On the other side of desk is the VR headset, so not only you can enjoy the best pc games but also emserse yourself in the virtual world.

The rest of the design of the room has been precisely designed in way to make you feel like you are in the ultimate gamer's paradise. From the gaming posters on the wall to the NES controller inspired rug to the minecraft torch lights, the furniture of this room is filled gaming industry inspired objects. If you ever get bored playing digital pixels there is also a classic arcade pinball machine to set your personal best record.
